## How I use it

- **Cadence** · weekly · sent EOD Friday to stakeholder distribution
- **Delivery** · team wiki (Confluence/Notion) · link shared in exec Slack
- **Tone** · exec-first (TL;DR) · data-backed (metrics) · action-oriented (decisions + next week)
- **Length** · 1 screen. Longer = hiding problems.

## Why this format works

1. **Stakeholders skim in 30 seconds** — TL;DR + health table tell the whole story
2. **Color + trend** — red alone doesn't distinguish "was red, still red" from "was green, now red"
3. **Decisions needed is explicit** — reports that end without asks become unread wallpaper
4. **Metrics vs. target AND vs. last week** — absolute numbers without context are noise
